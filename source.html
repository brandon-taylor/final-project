Ruthai's menus

Whether you're a regular or a new customer, eating Thai food is an experience in itself but can also be a challenge. Traditionally, Thais are known for preparing very spicy food. Well, rest assured, don't feel like you have to eat like you've been to Bangkok a million times. Go easy on yourself, we can cook to your needs. Just tell us if you like it MILD, MEDIUM, HOT, or RUTHAI HOT! Oh, and by the way, we do use the freshest produces and meats in the market.
