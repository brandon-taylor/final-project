Welcome to Ruthai's

Whether you're a regular or a new customer, eating Thai food is an experience in itself but can also be a challenge. Traditionally, Thai food is known for being very spicy. Well, rest assured, don't feel like you have to eat like you've been to Bangkok a million times. Go easy on yourself, we can cook to your needs. Just tell us if you like it MILD, MEDIUM, HOT, or RUTHAI HOT! Oh, and by the way, we do use the freshest produces and meats in the market.

About Us

Ruthai's Thai Kitchen opened its doors in the Spring of 2001 in Mt. Lookout. We have consistently been getting rave reviews about our style and taste in cooking authentic Thai food, the real way. So, if you've not dined with us before please do so soon because, yes, you're missing out! You can dine in or order take out. We have a great menu selection and an awesome Sushi bar prepared fresh by Chef Tim Tang himself. Come see why our new and regulars keep coming back!
